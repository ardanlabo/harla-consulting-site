About

An operational consulting firm built for real conditions.

Harla Consulting is focused on practical decision-support and analytical solutions for organizations operating across industry, supply chains, and humanitarian programs.

Who we are

Harla Consulting supports decision-makers who need clarity under constraints: imperfect data, multiple stakeholders, operational pressure, and accountability requirements.

We work at the intersection of operational execution and analytical structure—turning raw reporting into usable management systems and decision-ready outputs.

What we optimize for

  • Lean systems that teams actually use
  • Clear indicators tied to real decisions
  • Fast deployment, stable maintenance
